## Methodology Notes

### Correction Made
Initially, the analysis grouped by `SeriesName + Location + DayOfWeek`, which incorrectly treated the same series taught on different days as separate series. This inflated the series count to 52 and gave unrealistically high completeness percentages.

**Corrected grouping:** `SeriesName + Location` only
- Recognizes that the same series is taught multiple days per week
- Calculates expected lessons as: `weeks_span × classes_per_week`
- Provides accurate completeness percentages

### Key Insight
The Sheikh's teaching schedule is **extremely intensive**:
- Some series taught **every single day** of the week
- Most series taught 6 days per week
- This explains why we have ~30% completeness despite having 265 lessons
