## Key Points for Matching

### Location Distinction (CRITICAL)
- **جامع الورود** = Classes held at the masjid
- **عن بُعد** = Online classes (via Telegram channel)
- **Same series name with different location = DIFFERENT SERIES**
  - Example: تأسيس الأحكام at masjid (Sun/Mon) vs تأسيس الأحكام online (Wed)
  - Example: الأفنان الندية online (Sun/Mon) is one series

## Matching Strategy

When classifying a Telegram message:

1. **Parse the date** to determine day of week
2. **Check for location indicators:**
   - "عن بُعد" or "عبر قناة" = Online
   - Default = جامع الورود
3. **Look for series name** in the message text
4. **Match against this schedule** based on:
   - Day of week
   - Location (masjid vs online)
   - Series name mentioned
5. **If multiple series on same day**, use additional clues:
   - Time indicators (بعد العصر، بعد المغرب، بعد العشاء)
   - Chapter/topic names
   - Serial numbers if available
